SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Vendor: Acme Corporation
Client: ABC Company Finance Department

---

AGREEMENT TYPE: Technology Services Agreement

AGREEMENT TERM: 24 months from January 1, 2024 to December 31, 2025

SCOPE OF WORK:
- Provide enterprise software licensing and support services
- Include cloud infrastructure management and maintenance
- Deliver monthly maintenance updates and security patches
- Provide 24/7 technical support to end users
- Maintain system security and compliance standards
- Conduct quarterly performance reviews

SERVICE LEVELS AND PERFORMANCE STANDARDS:
- System uptime guarantee: 99.5% (measured monthly)
- Response time for critical issues: 4 hours
- Response time for high priority issues: 8 hours
- Response time for medium priority issues: 24 hours
- Issue resolution time for critical issues: 24 hours
- Issue resolution time for non-critical issues: 5 business days
- Scheduled maintenance windows: Maximum 2 hours per month, preferably weekends

PENALTIES FOR BREACH:
- SLA breach (uptime below 99.5%): Service credit of $500 per 0.5% below target
- Failure to respond within SLA: $250 per incident
- Missed quarterly review: $1,000 per occurrence
- Security breach: $10,000 and 30-day notice for contract termination

REPORTING OBLIGATIONS:
- Monthly uptime reports delivered by 5th business day of following month
- Quarterly business reviews with detailed performance metrics
- Annual security audit report by December 31st
- Incident reports within 24 hours of any critical issue
- Monthly patch deployment schedule provided 2 weeks in advance

SERVICING OBLIGATIONS:
- Maintain dedicated support team during business hours (8 AM - 6 PM EST)
- Provide 24/7 emergency support for critical incidents
- Implement patches within 72 hours of release
- Maintain backup and disaster recovery capabilities
- Conduct annual security assessments

KPIs AND VOLUME COMMITMENTS:
- Minimum customer satisfaction score: 90% (measured quarterly)
- Support ticket resolution rate: 95% within first contact
- Average response time target: 2 hours for all tickets
- Uptime achievement target: 99.5% monthly average
- Training delivery: Minimum 2 training sessions per quarter
- Innovation: Deliver 2 major feature updates annually

DATA SECURITY PROTOCOLS:
- ISO 27001 certification required and maintained
- SOC 2 Type II compliance mandatory
- Annual third-party security audits required
- Data encryption in transit: TLS 1.2 minimum
- Data encryption at rest: AES-256 minimum
- Multi-factor authentication for all admin access
- Regular penetration testing (minimum annually)
- Incident response plan required
- 30-day notice period for any security incidents

PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS:
- Monthly service fee: $10,000
- Invoicing: Monthly in advance on the 1st of each month
- Payment terms: Net 30 days from invoice date
- Annual price increase: Maximum 5% per year
- Price lock for first 12 months
- Volume discounts: Available for orders exceeding $200,000 annually

MILESTONE COMPLETION:
- Project kickoff: January 15, 2024
- Initial deployment: February 15, 2024
- First quarterly review: April 1, 2024
- System optimization phase: July 1, 2024
- Annual review and contract renewal discussion: December 1, 2024

DEPENDENCIES:
- Client must provide timely access to systems for updates
- Client must maintain network connectivity
- Client must perform data backups as per documented procedures
- Vendor requires 2 weeks notice for major configuration changes
- Client must appoint primary contact for vendor communication

BILLING STATUS:
- Monthly billing cycle
- Invoice due within 30 days
- Early payment discount: 2% if paid within 10 days
- Late payment penalty: 1.5% per month
- Annual reconciliation required

ADDITIONAL TERMS:
- Contract can be terminated with 60-day written notice
- Automatic renewal for 12-month periods unless notice given 90 days prior
- Early termination fee: 2 months of service fees if terminated before 12 months
- Liability cap: Limited to 12 months of fees paid
- Governing law: State of [Jurisdiction]
